About Us

Casco Kicks

CasCo Kicks is an e-commerce company that makes shopping for sneakerheads more convenient by offering services online such as the buying of pre-loved kicks as well as the sale of brand-new kicks together with limited-edition ones.

CasCo Kicks got their name from the surnames of its founders, Castro and Chanco, who came up with the idea together and started the company. It is a partnership company that offers sneakerheads and other people who are interested in keeping up to date on the newest arrivals access to an online marketplace platform. The company's target market consists of people who are interested in sneakers but do not have the time to check each brand for new releases and prefer to buy pre-loved or brand-new sneakers with less hassle. In a generation where technology is on the rise, CasCo Kicks is a place where you can buy brand-new shoes or sell used ones with just the click of a mouse.
